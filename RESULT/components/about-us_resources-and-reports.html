Annual Reports to the Community

We are excited to share the Medica Foundation Annual Community Reports. We hope you enjoy reading stories that feature the challenging and rewarding work of our nonprofit partners. It is an honor and privilege to support so many great organizations whose efforts help improve the health of the communities in which we live.

 

Meeting the Moment

As a nonprofit organization, we answer to our members and communities – not to shareholders. Our 2021 annual report, Meeting the Moment, tells the story of how we protected your health and responded to critical needs in the communities we serve.

2021 Annual Report

The Power of Community

Our 2020 annual report, The Power of Community, describes how we leaned on our values and relationships to protect the health and well-being of our health plan members and respond to the critical needs of our nonprofit community partners during a year of profound crisis. In it, you'll find details on Medica Foundation grants that aided COVID-19 response, supported communities affected by unrest, alleviated hunger, and advanced equity.

2020 Annual Report

The Power of Community

Medica, a nonprofit health plan, and Medica Foundation, a charitable grant making organization, are committed to understanding and responding to the unique concerns of communities we serve, building effective partnerships in those communities, and fostering innovative solutions that hold promise to improve health care quality and accessibility. Our annual report, The Power of Community, highlights our advocacy and impact in 2019.

2019 Annual Report

The Power of Giving

For us, giving well means several things. When we support community-based organizations, our focus is on understanding and empowering their work, and not imposing our agenda. It means funding organizations and programs that help people break out of toxic cycles and re-connect to sources of strength they may not have known about. It means building partnerships with organizations who demonstrate their ability to create lasting solutions to seemingly intractable problems. In this report, you will read stories about organizations who are doing all of these things to make sure people are able to achieve their best health.

2018 Annual Report

The Power Behind Change

Sometimes the challenges our communities face seem overwhelming, but people are resilient.  When smart people with compassionate hearts come together, and when they have the resources they need, the power of change is unleashed.  It is important to be reminded of how powerful that change can be, and of the ingredients – people, passion, resources, and community – that fuel change. That's why we offer these stories in this year's Annual Report. 

2017 Annual Report

Breaking Barriers

The Foundation's mission is clear and straightforward: improving people's health and removing barriers to getting health care. The path, however, is not so clear and straightforward. What it comes down to is the imperative to improve the health of those in need across differences in cultures and life experiences. That's the kind of work you'll read about in this report and that we are proud to support. 

2016 Annual Report

Our Future In Training

The promise of a brighter future is something many of us take for granted. But this promise is an illusion for many. It is not real or attainable. Circumstances like poverty, homelessness, mental health challenges, lack of access to support and care, and lack of timely response to a crisis all contribute to extinguishing the promise of a brighter future.  The grants we funded in 2015 were all about breaking the cycle and offering people the help they need to train for a different future. As you read this report, you’ll see themes emerge.

2015 Annual Report

Cultivating Connections

When it comes to change, it can never be a solo effort. Change is the result of the connections we make, and the success we have in cultivating relationships and providing resources to meet the varying needs within our communities. That’s why the Medica Foundation uses a multi-faceted funding approach to address these needs.

2014 Annual Report

Celebrating Ten Years

This year celebrates ten years of giving by the Medica Foundation. Over the years, Medica Foundation grants totaling $21 million have supported more than 750 organizations in our community, with awards ranging from $500 to $5 million. We are thankful for all the work our partners have done over the past ten years and look forward to supporting future efforts to improve the health and well being of our community.

2013 Annual Report

The Numbers Tell the Tale

We know that it is often difficult to get the most from our health care system. It can be fragmented and challenging to navigate. This can be especially true for people with lower incomes, with physical or mental disabilities, or whose cultural background doesn't equip them to deal effectively with the system. Our goal is to support those activities and programs that fill gaps, provide guidance, improve care and raise awareness.

2010 Annual Report

Victims of Circumstance. Or Not.

It's the lucky person who has abundant opportunities and favorable circumstances. For many, circumstances conspire to limit opportunities. The wings to fly are elusive for many of us. However, circumstances can be changed. The Medica Foundation is committed to funding programs and initiatives that strengthen the health of individuals and communities. That means finding innovative programs that don't take circumstances for granted, but work to change them so that people have the opportunity to thrive.

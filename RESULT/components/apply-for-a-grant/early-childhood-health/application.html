Step 2: Full review

We'll invite a selection of grantees for a full review that will include a site visit (either virtual or in-person). We'll notify organizations we select to move forward in the grant review process via email.

The email will include information for scheduling the visit along with a list of questions to discuss at the site visit. We may also request additional documentation before or after the visit, including clarification on financial position or updated grant outcomes.


Step 3: Notification of grant recipients

Our staff will contact organizations that receive funding to discuss grant project dates, payment schedule and required grant reports. We'll also send a grant agreement confirming these details.

We'll notify organizations via email if their project wasn't selected for funding. You're also welcome to contact us to discuss why a project didn't receive funding.

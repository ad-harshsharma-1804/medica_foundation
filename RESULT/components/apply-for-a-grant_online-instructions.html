Helpful Tip

Step Three – Complete the eligibility quiz and begin your application

After creating your account, you will be brought to the eligibility quiz. If you are eligible to apply for funding, you will be directed to the application. If you don't pass the quiz, please contact the Foundation   to verify you qualify for funding.

You can save your application and return to it later.  To access your application again you can use the “Return to an in-progress application” link on the same page where you clicked the link to start an application.  This will get you to the portal login page.

Attaching Documents

Required Documents

Required documents – Early Childhood Health, Behavioral Health, Strategic Initiative

Here is the list of documents we require you to submit with your online application.

  • Project budget
  • Organizational budget
  • Organization balance sheet
  • Audit statement
  • Board of directors list

    Note: You must submit a project and an organization budget with your online application. There is no required format for the budgets, but we do ask that you ensure they are readable as a PDF. If your organization is a government agency, please submit a document noting this status in lieu of the organization budget.


Required documents – Rural Health

  • Organization budget

    Note: You must submit an organization budget with your online application. There is no required format for the budgets, but we do ask that you ensure they are readable as a PDF. If your organization is a government agency, please submit the program budget most aligned with your request.


Uploading Attachments

You will upload your supporting documents within the application.

  1. Click the upload link.
  2. Click Browse and navigate to the correct attachment, then click Open.
  3. Repeat for any other required attachments.

Review and Submit Your Application

Finalize Application

Review

Review your application to ensure all required fields are complete.


Submit

  • After reviewing, click the Submit button. Ensure your application is complete prior to this; you cannot edit your application or attachments once you have clicked Submit.
  • Once you have submitted your application, you can expect a confirmation email with an application ID number.
  • If you do not receive an email confirmation, verify that your application has moved from "Draft" to "Submitted" in your account. You may also contact us for confirmation.


Helpful Tip

Be sure cookies are enabled on your internet browser. Remember to keep a record of your account login email and password.


Technical assistance will be available until 4:00 p.m. on the deadline dates. No assistance will be available after this time. Applications will be accepted until 11:59 p.m. on the deadline date.

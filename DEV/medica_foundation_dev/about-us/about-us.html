About us

Our history and vision

Established in 1992, the Medica Foundation is a nonprofit, charitable grant-making foundation for community-based programs and services. We're the charitable giving arm of Medica, a Minnesota-based health plan.

The Medica Foundation believes everyone deserves a fair and just opportunity to be as healthy as possible. Relationships drive our work as we engage our partners to address challenges that face the communities we serve. We're proud to share our statements of mission, vision, and values.


Mission

Improve the health of those in need by investing in community-led initiatives that advance health equity.


Vision

We envision healthy communities free of inequities, where health resources, care, and treatment are accessible and available to all.


Values

    COLLABORATION We encourage efforts that build partnerships and share expertise, resources, and responsibility to achieve a community's shared program goals and collective impact.
    COMMUNITY FOCUS We learn from locally based organizations and empower them to create change that improves the overall health within their community.
    EQUITY We are committed to moving our work forward in a respectful way to achieve optimal health outcomes that embrace race, culture, and language differences.
    INTEGRITY We hold ourselves accountable by operating to meet the highest ethical and professional standards in everything we do.
    STEWARDSHIP We carefully and responsibly manage the financial resources entrusted to us.

Principles

The Medica Foundation recognizes the importance of supporting the unique health needs of individual communities in our grantmaking.


Our Overarching principles:

  • Benefit the communities Medica serves
  • Advance health equity
  • Build trust and collaboration with grantee partners and create mutual accountability between funders and nonprofits
  • Improve the availability of, access to, and/or quality of health care
  • Achieve success or demonstrate effectiveness through measurable outcomes

We give priority to programs that:

  • Align with our overarching principles
  • Address community issues and support community-led initiatives
  • Promote equitable solutions to advance health equity
  • Have a high likelihood of improved health through sustainable impact
  • Build partnerships and collaborations within the community

 

The Team

The Medica Foundation is governed by a Board of Directors. The Board and Foundation leadership team meet each year to review community needs and past grant outcomes to determine the annual funding strategy. Funding areas are modified from year to year based on community needs.

Rural Health Grants


Minnesota funding only.

Individual grants may be awarded up to $10,000 annually.

Application opens July 1, 2025.


We aim to fund community-led solutions that address health equity in rural communities. 

We prioritize proposals that address:

  • Culturally-focused care
  • Dental health
  • Domestic violence and sexual assault services and prevention
  • Independent living for seniors
  • Maternal and birth health
  • Mental health and chemical health
  • Primary care for underserved communities
  • Sexual health
  • Transportation for medical/health appointments

Eligibility:

  • Rural Health grant recipients are eligible to submit an application for funding in consecutive years.
  • Funding is limited to organizations based outside of the seven-county Twin Cities metropolitan area.
  • Organizations seeking to do work within our service area but do not have a physical presence in Greater Minnesota are ineligible.
  • Funding requests for events, sponsorships, seminars, museums, camps, and general health and wellness activities including nutrition, exercise, etc., are ineligible.

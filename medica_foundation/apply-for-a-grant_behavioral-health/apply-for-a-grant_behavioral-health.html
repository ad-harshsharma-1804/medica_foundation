Behavioral Health Grants


Minnesota funding only.

NOTE: As we shift to a two-year grant cycle, we will not be accepting new behavioral health grant applications in 2025. We'll reopen this funding area in 2026.


We seek proposals for programs that improve the health outcomes for people experiencing or at risk of serious mental illness and/or substance use disorders. We prioritize proposals that provide culturally-responsive care and strategically align their work to advance health equity.

We prioritize proposals for programs that improve the mental and chemical health of individuals by addressing:

  • Behavioral health workforce
  • Co-occurring disorders
  • Integration of physical and behavioral health care
  • Service delivery gaps
  • Supportive and intensive case management services

Eligibility:

  • We provide behavioral health grant funding to support chemical or mental health. Programming that addresses the lifestyle behaviors of individuals doesn't meet our criteria and is ineligible.
  • Please note that these are program grants. Although we do not provide fully unrestricted funding, our grants can be used for expenses needed to carry out the program, including staff and a portion for overhead.
  • If you have any questions about our behavioral health grants, please contact Elyse Coleman
